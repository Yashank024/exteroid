About EXTEROID

Simplifying Data Management for Everyone

Our Mission

At EXTEROID, we believe that data management shouldn't require a degree in computer science. Our mission is to provide powerful, professional-grade tools that are accessible to everyone—completely free of charge.

Whether you're trying to extract a table from a screenshot, merge messy Excel files, or clean up a contact list, EXTEROID automates the tedious parts of your workflow so you can focus on what matters.

Privacy First

We understand the sensitivity of your data. That's why EXTEROID is built as a client-side tool. This means your Excel files and images are processed directly in your web browser.

We do not store your files. Once you close the tab, your data is gone from our interface. You get the power of cloud computing with the privacy of a local application.
