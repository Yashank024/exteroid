Introduction

At EXTEROID, accessible from our website, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by EXTEROID and how we use it.

Data Processing & Security

EXTEROID operates as a client-side web application. This means:

  • No File Storage: When you use our Image to Data, Excel Consolidator, or Excel Automation tools, your files are processed locally within your web browser. We do not upload your Excel files or images to our servers for permanent storage.
  • Temporary Processing: Any data extraction happens in real-time. Once you refresh the page or close the browser tab, the data is cleared from your browser's memory.
  • No Data Retention: We do not maintain copies of your files on any server. Your data never leaves your device.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, EXTEROID uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of a third-party vendor on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to our website and other sites on the internet. However, visitors may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at the following URL:
