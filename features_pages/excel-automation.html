Free Online Excel Automation Tool

DataOps Pro offers a powerful, free online Excel automation solution that helps you clean, format, merge, and organize your spreadsheets without any complex software. Whether you're an accountant, student, or business owner, our tool simplifies your data workflow.

Key Features:

  • Remove Duplicates: Instantly find and remove duplicate rows based on selected columns.
  • Merge & Split: Combine multiple columns into one or split text across new columns.
  • Data Cleaning: Trim extra spaces, fix text case, and standardize formatting.
  • Advanced Formulas: Apply SUM, COUNTIF, VLOOKUP, and more with our no-code builder.
